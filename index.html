Mission

The Fountain of Life Church is a ministry with a mission to provide a home for the lost; guide those in search of direction, purpose and calling; and raise a people who are committed to making a difference.

Vision

The Fountain of Life Church exists to teach men and women the art of building successful relationships and the principles of leadership so they can be all they were created to be.

Ministry

With the help of the Holy Spirit, we strive to NURTURE and DEVELOP all to the full potential of their callings, thereby raising them to be the leaders they are called to be in their different places of life assignments.

Our Beliefs

  • We believe in one God - Father, Son and Holy Spirit, Creator of all things.
  • We believe that the Lord Jesus Christ, the only begotten Son of God, was conceived of the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, and was crucified for the sins of mankind; He died, was buried and rose from the dead on the third day. He ascended into heaven and is now seated at the right hand of God the Father and is true God and true man.
  • We believe in the Bible in its entirety to be the inspired Word of God and the infallible rule of faith and conduct.
  • We believe in the resurrection of the dead, the eternal joy of the saved, and the eternal punishment of the lost.
  • We believe in sanctification through the Word of God and by the Holy Spirit, and we believe in personal holiness, purity of heart and life.
  • We believe in water baptism, in the baptism in the Holy Spirit as distinct from the New Birth, and evidenced by speaking with tongues as the Spirit of God gives utterance (Acts 2:4).
  • We believe in the gifts of the Spirit, and the evidence of the fruit of the Spirit. We believe all these are available to believers.
  • We believe in the Christian's hope - the soon coming, personal return of the Lord Jesus Christ.

Meet Our Pastors

Tolulope Ige is a seasoned Architectural Technologist and Building performance expert.

Prior to founding ALD studios in 2018, He worked in Amarch Consultants for 10 years as an Architectural Technologist and later as a project Architect with many completed building projects.

He is the founding director of ALD studios: a firm of seasoned Arch. Technologist, Architects and Structural Engineers. He provides advisory services to many reputable property firms in Nigeria amongst these firms is MGC Limited: A property development in Lagos.

He is a member of the Nigeria Institute of Architects

He's a teacher of Grace and has a mandate and passion to raise fire and light bearer leaders. He's a teacher to many and a father to his biological children and spiritual children.

Pastor Tolulope and his wife, Olufunke are both ordained pastors at The fountain of life church and they lead The Grace Family of The Fountain of Life Church under the impeccable and amiable leadership of Pastor Taiwo Odukoya.

Pastor Tolulope Ige

Olufunke Ige is the Principal Consultant at The Reconciler Company. She is a Life and Family coach, Emotional Intelligence Practitioner, Cognitive Behavioral therapist, Author, Teacher and Counselor. She is expert in pre and post marriage counselling.

Being a first-hand receiver of the consequences of a separated home, Olufunke with her siblings provided lasting solutions to her parents broken marriage in her teenage years. This experience forged a strong passion for reconciling home with a firm determination that no one goes through the same pain she underwent. She has since then had opportunities to reconciling families starting from her university days as the fellowship president in the university. With over sixteen years of experience, she has become the light in restructuring and re-finding the true meaning of family. A good family is a good nation. In 2018 alone, she reached about A THOUSAND families across the globe using both physical and virtual platforms.

She has authored articles and books; Sex and Spirituality and Emotional makeover. Olufunke Tolulope Ige also serves on the board of directors of NGOs, Churches and Corporate organizations. She is married to Architect Tolulope Ige and a mother to many biological and non-biological children.

She is the founder of Olufunke Tolulope Ige Ministries with different functional arms with OTIM foundation, raising David and Deborah, Faith Foundation Publications and Fire Conference. and convener with A fire brand Team of The Fire Conference.
